Our Story

A Legacy of
Taste & Warmth

Nestled in the heart of Bagalkot district, Karnataka, Nihal Family Restaurant was born from a passion for authentic, home-style Indian cuisine. We believe that food is more than sustenance — it is memory, culture, and love served on a plate.

From aromatic biryanis cooked in dum to sizzling tandoori preparations, every dish at Nihal's is crafted with fresh local ingredients and generations-old recipes that carry the soul of Karnataka's rich culinary tradition.
